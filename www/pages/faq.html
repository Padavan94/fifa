FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I tried to deposit and the money came off my debit or credit card but is not in my account?

Under no circumstance do we charge a card or Paypal account without consent.

How much does it cost me to play?

CASHFORFIFA works as a secured third party for online gamers to set up head 2 head tournaments. Once a tournament has been accepted by both members, money from both accounts is automatically deposited into our neutral account. After the online game has been played, the results have been reported and verified by each player, the winner's account will automatically be credited.

What video games does CASHFORFIFA support?

If you can play a head 2 head match online, then we support it. A list of all our available games can be found under choose a platform section on the homepage. We are also always open to suggestions.

How do I deposit money into my GamerSaloon.com account?

You can either deposit money using your verified PayPal account, credit, debit card or pre paid gift card, you can also send us a money order. We offer online payment through PayPal. You can deposit money through the cashier page. You can also make payments by mail, mail your money order along with a note of your name, address, phone number and CASHFORFIFA username to:
Beyond Gaming, LLC
1229 South Washington Ave.
Royal Oak, MI 48067
CASHFORFIFA will never charge your card without your consent for any reason.
There is a $10 minimum on all deposits.
