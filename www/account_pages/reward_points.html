REWARD POINTS

Rewards Points Program allows members to earn Bonus Cash and prizes by watching videos, completing surveys, and responding to offers from our partners and Premium members to earn just for playing on the site.

All Members can go to the offers page and browse dozens of online offers. By completing these offers you can earn Reward Points.

Premium Members typically will receive 1 point for every $1 of 1 vs. 1 matches they participate in. For example a $20 1 vs. 1 match of Madden earns a Premium member 20 rewards points. The member earns the points regardless of if they win or lose as long as they report or verify the outcome of the match.

In order to earn your rewards points for play you must be a Premium Member in good standing and you must either report or verify the results of your match. If your opponent reports the results of the match and you let the system auto-verify the match you will not receive any reward points.As long as you are a member in good standing, rewards points can be collected and redeemed for the prizes below.
